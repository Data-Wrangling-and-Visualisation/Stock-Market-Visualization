Russian Stock Market Analysis

About This Visualization

This interactive visualization explores the relationship between daily and yearly returns of major Russian companies, with additional insights into market capitalization.

Key Features

  • Bubble size represents market capitalization
  • Color gradient shows company size variation
  • Interactive tooltips with detailed information
  • Real-time correlation coefficient calculation
  • Responsive design for different screen sizes

How to Interact

  • Hover over bubbles to see company details
  • Observe the correlation coefficient in the top-right
  • Note the concentration of large-cap companies
